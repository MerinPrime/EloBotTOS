This Privacy Policy explains how TBC Team (The Bonk Collective) ("we," "us"), operated by Discord contact .mxxr1n, collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use EloBot.

1. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests:

  • Email: fotisikzxc@gmail.com
  • Discord: .mxxr1n

2. Information We Collect

Depending on features used, we may process:

  • Discord account data (user ID, username, display name);
  • Bonk.io profile data (nickname, account level, and selected account skin);
  • server/channel/message metadata needed to run commands and bot features;
  • command and interaction content submitted to EloBot;
  • moderation and security logs (timestamps, action type, abuse indicators);
  • technical diagnostics (errors, rate-limit events, anti-abuse telemetry).

We do not intentionally collect passwords or payment card information.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • provide and maintain EloBot functionality;
  • detect, prevent, and investigate abuse, spam, raids, and security incidents;
  • troubleshoot and improve service reliability;
  • enforce our Terms and comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Where GDPR-style legal bases apply, we rely on:

  • performance of a service contract;
  • legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention, service integrity);
  • legal obligations;
  • consent, where required.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only:

  • with hosting/infrastructure providers acting on our instructions;
  • when required by law or lawful authority;
  • to protect rights, safety, and platform integrity.

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes described above. Typical periods:

  • operational logs: up to 90 days;
  • security and abuse logs: up to 12 months;
  • longer retention if required by law or for active dispute/security handling.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of your data. To submit a request, contact us at fotisikzxc@gmail.com.

EloBot is not directed to children below Discord's minimum age or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction. If we learn we processed data unlawfully from a minor, we will delete it.
